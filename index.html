FAQ

Quick answers to the questions that affect scope, pricing, and schedule.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

For projects that require permitting, we’ll outline what’s required during the walkthrough and coordinate the process as part of the scope.

Do you subcontract plumbing and electrical?

Most remodels require licensed trades. If your project needs them, we’ll coordinate and schedule them as part of the plan.

What information helps you estimate fastest?

Your town/zip, rough budget range, desired start window, and a few photos of the existing space.

How far out are you booking?

It depends on scope and season. Include your start window in the request and we’ll give you a realistic schedule expectation.
